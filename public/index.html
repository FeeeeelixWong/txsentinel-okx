PRODUCT RUNBOOK

One action, four controlled stages

Policy logic stays offchain. Wallet authority stays with the user. Evidence is anchored onchain.

  1. 01 Describe action

    Chain, operation, recipient, amount, and supplied simulation evidence.

  2. 02 Evaluate policy

    Deterministic rules return ALLOW, HOLD, or DENY with exact reasons.

  3. 03 Confirm in wallet

    OKX Wallet remains the only signing boundary. TxSentinel never receives a key.

  4. 04 Anchor evidence

    X Layer stores the policy revision, action digest, decision, and receipt hash.
