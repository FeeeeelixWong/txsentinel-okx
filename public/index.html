PRODUCT RUNBOOK

One action, four explicit stages

Policy logic stays offchain. Wallet authority stays with the user. Evidence is anchored onchain.

  1. 01 Describe action

    Chain, operation, recipient, amount, and supplied simulation evidence.

  2. 02 Route with preflight

    Get READY, REVIEW, or BLOCKED without payment, a formal decision, or receipt hashes.

  3. 03 Purchase formal evidence

    Valid requests use OKX x402 to return ALLOW, HOLD, or DENY with reasons and stable hashes.

  4. 04 Act or anchor

    The wallet may execute an allowed action; a paid receipt may be anchored on X Layer.
